Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How long does delivery take? ▼
Dhaka Metro: next business day. Rest of Bangladesh: 2-4 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order ships.
What sizes are available? ▼
We carry S, M, L, XL, and XXL. Our tees are oversized fit — if you prefer regular fit, size down one.
How do I pay? ▼
We accept bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and Cash on Delivery. For COD, a small advance may be required for orders above ৳2000.
Can I return or exchange? ▼
Yes! 7-day exchange policy. Defect or wrong item: replacement at no cost. Size exchange accepted within 7 days (unused, unwashed).
How do I place a custom order? ▼
Message us on Facebook with your design idea and quantity. We send a mockup within 24 hours. Minimum: 1 piece.
How do I care for my tee? ▼
Machine wash cold, inside out. Tumble dry low or hang dry. Do not bleach. Iron inside out on medium heat.
